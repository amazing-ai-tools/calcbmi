# Communication Marketing — calcbmi

## Positioning
calcbmi should be described as a quick BMI calculator, not as a medical product.

## Messaging Rules
- Say what the app does now.
- Do not claim diagnosis, personalized medical advice, or treatment guidance.
- Mention BMI limitations plainly.
- Keep copy concise and useful.
- Replace the default BugZero starter copy once the calculator UI exists.

## Useful Angles
- Fast BMI estimate.
- Metric and imperial unit support.
- Clear category explanation.
- No account required.
- Built-in feedback through BugZero.
